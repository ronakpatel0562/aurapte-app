Responding to a Situation (RTS)
--- Situation 1 ---
Prompt: You are going out of town for three days, and you have a pet cat at home. You want your friend Monica to feed your cat while you're away. Leave a voice message for Monica explaining the situation. In your message: Tell her when you’re leaving and returning., Ask her to feed your cat. Give any specific instructions (e.g. food type, feeding times). Thank her for her help.
Sample Answer:
Hi Monica, I hope you are doing well. I want to share about a situation in which I’m at this moment. I am going out of town for three days, and I have a pet cat at home. I want you to feed my cat while I am away. I assure you that this will not repeat again and I would be more careful in the future. I really realize the importance of this situation and I am hopeful for a positive outcome. Thank you for Understanding.
